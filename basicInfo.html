Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Adventures of Tom Sawyer
  • Author
  • Mark Twain
  • Published
  • 1876, American Publishing Company.
  • Genre
  • The novel fits many genres, primarily episodic quest, bildungsroman, and satire.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in St. Petersburg, Missouri in the 1840's or 50's.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is told from the third person from an unspecified adult narrator, as he follows Tom Sawyer's adventures in his youth.
  • Conflict
  • There is two main conflicts in the novel. First the conflict between Tom and Huck against Injun Joe, who they believe is out to murder them after they discovered his gold. The other between Tom and the constrictions he feels from adult society.
