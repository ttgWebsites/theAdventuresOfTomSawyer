Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • The Adventures of Tom Sawyer chronicles a young boy, Tom's, adventures in his small river town in Missouri. Tom likes to cause trouble and is quite the prankster. He is also very street smart, and at one point tricks some of the boys in town to pay him to do his yard work. Tom develops a friendship with another boy, Huckleberry Finn, and the two embark on nighttime adventures. On one occasion they witness a murder, and swear themselves to secrecy. The story tells of their experiences following the murder.
