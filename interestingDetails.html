Interesting Details

  • The protagonist from the novel, Tom, is seen in another three published books by Twain, one of the novels however focuses on the character Huck Finn, from this novel, and Tom only plays a minor role.
  • Twain grew up in Hannibal, MO, and sets this novel in a very similar place, as well as bases many of the adventures off adventures he experienced as a young boy.
  • Mark Twain is just the author's pen name, in fact, his real name is Samuel Clemens.
