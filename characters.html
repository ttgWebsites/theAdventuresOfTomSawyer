Characters

  • Tom Sawyer
  • Protagonist
  • Has a vivid imagination and always gets himself into mischief that he has to get himself and his friends out of
  • Is very moral and has a good heart
  • Becomes a leader as the novel goes on
  • Aunt Polly
  • Tom Sawyers aunt and guardian.
  • Tries to be warm and kind towards Tom while also trying to be the disciplinarian.
  • Known as a very nice person in the novel that just wants to be loved by Tom
  • Huckleberry Finn
  • Is envied by the local boys in town for the amount of freedom that he has
  • Son of town drunk
  • Very superstitious and disliked by the respected people of the community
  • Replaces Tom's best friend, Joe Harper, as his partner in his adventures
  • Becky Thatcher
  • Judge Thatcher's daughter
  • Is loved the minute she moves to town for her beauty
  • She tries to make Tom jealous which makes him do the same back to her
  • Joe Harper
  • Tom's best friend
  • Inseparable as friends and Twain refers to them as “Two souls with but a single thought”
  • When Joe gets homesick on Jackson's island, Huck takes his place
  • Sid
  • Tom's half brother
  • Likes getting Tom into trouble
  • Opposite of Tom, mean but acts like he is a perfect child
  • Mary
  • Tom's cousin that likes him a lot.
  • Similar to Sid but acts like an angel when she actually is one
  • Injun Joe
  • A violent murderer
  • Plants to violently attack the Widow Douglas
  • Is half Indian and half White, he is motivated by revenge because he is excluded in society mainly because of his race
  • Muff Potter
  • Friends with Injun Joe
  • Unfortunate drunk
  • Nice to Tom and Huck who bring him a present when he gets thrown in jail for allegedly killing Dr. Robinson
  • Tries to get tom to tell the jury at Potter's trial who actually killed Dr. Robinson
  • Dr. Robinson
  • The local doctor who is respected by many in the town
  • Hires Injun Joe and Muff to dig up the grave of Hoss Williams so he can use the body for medical experiments.
  • Mr. Sprague
  • Minister of the town church
  • The Widow Douglas
  • Nice resident of the community that is friendly with the children
  • Gives Tom and Becky ice cream and allows them to sleep at her house
  • Nice to Huck since before she found out he saved her life
  • Mr. Jones
  • Lives near the Widow Douglas' house with his sons
  • Helps Huck recover after he stops Injun Joe from attacking the widow
  • Judge Thatcher
  • Becky's dad
  • Judge for the county
  • Celebrity of the community
  • Does noble things in the community like take care of the treasure money the boys find and close the caves because they are unsafe
  • Jim
  • The slave of Aunt Polly
  • Amy Lawrence
  • A girl that Tom used to love before he met Becky Thatcher
  • Ben Rogers
  • A friend of Tom's who Tom has paint his aunt's fence
  • Alfred Temple
  • A boy that Becky pretends to like to make Tom Jealous
  • Mr. Walters
  • The towns Sunday School teacher
  • Tries to make Judge Thatcher happy by giving Tom a bible that he has not earned
  • Mr. Dobbins
  • Schoolmaster
  • Sad character that is upset that he did not fulfill his goal of becoming a doctor.
  • Now gets pranked by the boys of the school
